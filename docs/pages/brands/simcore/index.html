Interactive Scientific Computing Platform

SimCore is an interactive platform for learning and exploring physics, materials, and scientific computing through hands-on simulations, rich visualizations, and guided modules.

Key Capabilities

SimCore ships with a growing collection of quantum, solid-state, and statistical physics modules — including band structure visualizations, time-dependent Schrödinger solvers, Ising models, Brownian motion, and more.

Quantum & solid-state modules Interactive visualizations Educational workflows Supabase-backed data

Audience

Built for students, researchers, and practitioners who want to explore complex physical systems through modern web tooling, without installing heavy desktop software.
